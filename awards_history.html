CCA History


The Modern Dance Co-Curricular Activity (CCA) was established in 2021 with the aim of providing students a creative platform to express themselves through contemporary and urban dance styles. What began with a small group of passionate dancers has grown into a vibrant and dynamic community.

Over the years, our team has participated in numerous school events, external competitions, and workshops, consistently impressing audiences with our energy, teamwork, and originality. Highlights include our performances in the annual Arts Showcase and achieving podium finishes in inter-college dance battles.

More than just dance, our CCA focuses on building confidence, discipline, and camaraderie among members. As we continue to evolve, we remain committed to nurturing talent, embracing innovation, and sharing the joy of dance with the wider school community.
