Terms of Service

By participating in the Modern Dance CCA (Co-Curricular Activity), you agree to comply with the following terms and expectations. These guidelines are designed to ensure a safe, respectful, and productive experience for all members.

1. Participation & Commitment

  • All members are expected to attend scheduled practices, rehearsals, and performances unless a valid excuse is provided in advance.
  • Consistent absenteeism without valid reasons may result in exclusion from performances or removal from the CCA.
  • Members must commit to teamwork and contribute positively to the group.

2. Code of Conduct

  • Respect instructors, student leaders, and fellow members at all times.
  • Follow school rules and behave responsibly during CCA sessions and events.
  • Bullying, harassment, or disruptive behavior will not be tolerated.

3. Attire & Safety

  • Wear appropriate attire (e.g., T-shirt, long pants) for every practice.
  • Bring water and stay hydrated during sessions.
  • Inform your instructor immediately if you are feeling unwell or injured.

4. Health & Medical Consent

By participating, you confirm that you are medically fit for physical activity. If you have any existing conditions or injuries, you must inform your teacher or CCA coordinator beforehand.

5. Use of Media & Recordings

Photos or videos taken during practices or performances may be used for school publications, social media, or documentation. If you or your parent/guardian do not consent to this, please notify the CCA coordinator in writing.

6. Performance Expectations

  • All members must fully participate in preparations leading up to any school showcase, competition, or public performance.
  • Be punctual, prepared, and cooperative during rehearsals and events.

7. Amendments to Terms

The school or CCA teacher-in-charge may revise these terms as needed. Any changes will be communicated in advance.
