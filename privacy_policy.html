Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how personal information collected through the Modern Dance CCA (Co-Curricular Activity) will be used, stored, and protected. By participating in the CCA, you and your parent/guardian agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information for administrative and safety purposes:

  • Student name, class, and contact details
  • Parent/guardian contact information
  • Attendance records and participation history
  • Photographs or video recordings from CCA activities
  • Health-related declarations (e.g., allergies or physical limitations)

2. Purpose of Collection

The information is collected to:

  • Manage student participation in Modern Dance CCA
  • Ensure student safety and well-being
  • Communicate with parents or guardians
  • Document performances, practices, and CCA achievements
  • Promote CCA activities through school-approved platforms (with consent)

3. Photo and Video Use

Photos or videos may be used in school newsletters, websites, yearbooks, or CCA promotional materials. If you do not wish for your child to appear in such media, please notify us through contact us or email moderndancenyp@gmail.com.

4. Data Protection & Storage

All collected data will be stored securely by the school and accessed only by authorized personnel. Physical and digital safeguards are in place to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share student information with third parties unless required by school policy, educational authorities, or legal obligations.

6. Student Rights

Students and parents/guardians have the right to:

  • Request access to the information collected
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Withdraw consent for use of photographs or videos

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in school policy or legal requirements. Any updates will be communicated to participants in a timely manner.

8. Contact Information
